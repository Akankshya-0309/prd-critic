PRD Critic

An AI-powered PRD reviewer that scores drafts against a 9-criterion senior-PM rubric and surfaces the two or three priority fixes worth making before the review meeting.

Try the prototype → Read the case study

The question I was exploring

Generic LLMs give agreeable, vague PRD feedback. A senior PM doesn't — they quote the draft, prioritize the two biggest issues, and tell you what to fix by the review meeting. I wanted to see how far a well-designed rubric and system prompt could close that gap.

How I approached it

  • Wrote the rubric first. Nine weighted criteria, each with 1–5 anchors. The rubric is the product; the prompt is the delivery.
  • Built a system prompt that forces quoting. Every score must cite the PRD directly — no free-floating opinions, no hallucinated research.
  • Tested on 10 PRDs (three deliberately designed: weak, medium, strong) and recorded where the critic over-called or under-called.
  • Documented failure modes so I could talk about the tool's limits honestly, not just its wins.
